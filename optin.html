SMS Terms & Consent

Information about how RH Health Inc uses text messaging to communicate with patients about their care.

Consent to Receive SMS Messages

RH Health Inc receives patient contact information through contracted healthcare providers, facilities, and partner organizations who have obtained patient consent for care coordination and service delivery.

How We Obtain Consent: Patients provide consent to their primary healthcare provider or facility for care coordination services. Contact information is shared with RH Health Inc under Business Associate Agreements (BAA) and service contracts. Patients are informed that RH Health Inc may contact them regarding their care as part of the services provided by their healthcare provider.

Consent Framework: By receiving care through our partner organizations, patients consent to care coordination communications from RH Health Inc as part of their treatment plan, including appointment scheduling and reminders, health updates, medication reminders, and communications necessary for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations (TPO) under HIPAA.

Types of Messages You May Receive

  • Appointment Reminders and Confirmations: Notifications about scheduled visits and care appointments
  • Test Results and Care Updates: Important information about your lab results and health status
  • Medication Reminders: Alerts to help you stay on track with your prescriptions and refills
  • Care Coordination and Follow-up Notifications: Messages to ensure continuity of your care
  • Service-Related Communications: Important updates about your healthcare services

Message Frequency & Rates

Message frequency varies based on your care needs and appointments. Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan. RH Health Inc does not charge for messages, but your carrier's standard rates apply.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message from RH Health Inc
  • Contacting RH Health Inc directly at info@robinhoodhealth.co
  • Requesting opt-out through your primary care provider

For help or questions, reply HELP to any message or contact our support team.

Privacy & Legal Basis

Your Privacy Matters: Your phone number will only be used for care-related communications from RH Health Inc. We follow strict HIPAA guidelines to protect your health information. Your contact information is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis: Communications are conducted under HIPAA Treatment, Payment, and Healthcare Operations (TPO) provisions, with proper Business Associate Agreements in place with referring providers and facilities. We maintain records of all communications for HIPAA and TCPA compliance purposes.
