Frequently Asked Questions

Am I eligible for this program?+

If you have Medicaid or are part of a participating health plan, you may be eligible. Contact us to find out.

How much does this cost?+

For eligible members, all services are completely free. We work with your health plan to cover all costs.

How does the home testing work?+

We mail you a test kit with simple instructions. Complete the test at home and mail it back in the prepaid package. A doctor reviews your results within 24 hours.
