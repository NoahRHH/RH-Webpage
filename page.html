Frequently Asked Questions

Am I eligible for this program?+

If you have Medicaid or are part of a participating health plan, you may be eligible for free at-home health screenings. Your healthcare provider can tell you if you qualify.

How much does this cost?+

For eligible members, all services are completely free. We work with your health plan to cover all costs.

Is my information private?+

Yes. We follow all HIPAA regulations and share your results only with your healthcare providers to ensure you get the best care possible.

What happens after I take the test?+

A doctor reviews your results within 24 hours. We share the results with your primary care provider and help schedule any follow-up care you might need.
