Our Story

Why Robinhood Health Exists

We discovered a troubling truth: Medicaid members consistently receive lower quality preventive care than those with commercial insurance. The numbers speak for themselves.

This isn't just about statistics – it's about real people missing critical health screenings that could save their lives.

We founded Robinhood Health to change this. By combining smart technology with compassionate care, we're ensuring every Medicaid member gets the preventive care they deserve.
