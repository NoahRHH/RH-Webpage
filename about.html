We're closing healthcare gaps for Medicaid members by bringing essential health screenings directly to your home.

Why We Started Robinhood Health

We saw a troubling reality: Medicaid members consistently receive lower quality care than those with commercial insurance. Whether it's cancer screenings, diabetes management, or kidney health, the gaps are real and they're costing lives.

That's not right. Everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, regardless of their insurance type or where they live.

We founded Robinhood Health to change this. By combining smart technology with genuine human care, we're making it easier than ever for Medicaid members to get the preventive care they need – right from home.

Our Mission

To ensure every Medicaid member gets the same quality of preventive care as anyone else – delivered with dignity, convenience, and zero cost.

What Makes Us Different
