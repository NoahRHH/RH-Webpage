Careers at Robinhood Health

Small team. Real ownership. Work that reaches the people the system skips.

We're a virtual medical group that gets high-risk Medicaid patients the preventive screenings they keep missing—and we run the whole thing for our partners. We move fast and we're small, so whoever takes one of these on will own real work from day one.
