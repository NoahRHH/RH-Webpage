SHERWOOD PLATFORM

Full-Stack Care Delivery

Think of it like aviation autopilot. The system handles routine work. Your physicians keep authority over clinical decisions.

1

Detection

Our algorithms scan your entire patient panel daily, identifying every care gap in real-time. No manual chart reviews. No patients falling through the cracks.

2

Protection

Automatic nudges reach patients before care is missed. SMS, email, and AI-powered conversations that feel personal because they know each patient's history.

3

Coordination

At-home test kits ship automatically. Results come back. Our physicians review within 24 hours. Everything flows directly to your EMR.

4

Resolution

We maintain accountability until treatment is complete. Specialist appointments, follow-ups, prescriptions handled. The gap doesn't close until the patient gets care.
